I would consider myself a writer and editor with backgrounds in photography and videography. After working on my high school yearbook as a writer for one year, I moved up to editor my junior year and found my love for writing and editing, ultimately leading me to major in multipltaform journalism with a concentration in film at the University of Maryland- College Park, where I will be graduating from in May 2022.
