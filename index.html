paulina duque

Montgomery County born and raised, writing at Maryland is where Paulina spent most of her days. The daughter of two Colombian immigrants, Paulina Duque grew up on the east coast but made frequent visits to Colombia growing up, shaping her vision, inspirations, passions, and draws creativity and ideas from her personal experiences. Paulina is a writer and editor with backgrounds in photography and videography. After working on her high school yearbook as a writer during her first year, she moved up to editor during her junior year which is when she truly found her love for writing and editing, ultimately leading her to major in multiplatform journalism with a concentration in film at the University of Maryland- College Park, where she will be graduating from in May 2022.
